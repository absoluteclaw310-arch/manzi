Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our contact forms or direct communication. This typically includes your name, email address, phone number, and details about your remodeling project.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for the purpose of providing professional remodeling services, including:

  • Providing free estimates and project consultations.
  • Communicating with you regarding project timelines and details.
  • Scheduling site visits and installations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is used only for the administration of your remodeling project and is not shared outside of our operations except when required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we have on file for you or to ask us to remove your information from our records. To do so, please contact us via the contact form on our homepage.
