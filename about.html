About Manzi Remodeling

At Manzi Remodeling, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create living spaces that are as beautiful as they are functional.

As a Remodeling Pro with 25+ yrs of experience in the Capital District of NY, our team is dedicated to honest work, clear communication, and a relentless pursuit of perfection in every tile laid and every cabinet installed.

We treat every project as if it were in our own home, ensuring that the final result is something we can both be proud of.
